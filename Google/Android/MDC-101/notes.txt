Material Components (MDC) help developers implement Material Design.

In this codelab, you'll build a login page for Shrine that contains:
1. Two text fields, one for entering a username and the other for a password
2. Two buttons, one for "Cancel" and one for "Next"
3. The name of the app (Shrine)
4. An image of Shrine's logo
